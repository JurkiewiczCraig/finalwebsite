Showcasing the EPA's Walkability Index

For Citizens and City Planners alike

Ideal

Create a user friendly interactive web platform that display and inform members of the public on factors that are involved with creating a pedestrian friendly walkable city

Reality

The general public doesn’t have all the information when it comes to all the factors that go into influencing walkability

Consequences

Without public knowledge surrounding factors that go into creating a pedestrian friendly city, the public won’t be as informed when city officials make changes to policy

Proposal

to create an interactive web applications and inform the public of factors that are put into consideration when creating a walkability index

Technology Explored

AGOL, ArcGIS Dashboards, Storymaps
